FitTrack Design Direction

A unified, mobile-first design system focused on creating a clean, motivating, and efficient user experience.

Design Philosophy

The chosen direction is a **Clean, Card-Based Dashboard with Mobile-First Responsiveness**. It utilizes a modern aesthetic with a strong emphasis on scannable, card-based layouts. The interface is data-driven, presenting key metrics, progress bars, and activity lists to give users an immediate sense of their progress and keep them engaged.
